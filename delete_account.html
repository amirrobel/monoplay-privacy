Account Deletion Request — Monoplay

App: Monoplay

Contact email: monoplay.app@gmail.com

How to delete your account

To request deletion of your Monoplay account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to monoplay.app@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. In the email body include:
    • Your in-game nickname
    • Your Monoplay Display ID (visible in Profile → Settings)
    • The email address linked to your Google or Apple account used for login
  4. We will process your request within 14 days and confirm completion by email.

What data is deleted

Upon request, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Account profile (nickname, avatar, level, XP)
  • Game statistics and match history
  • Friends list and in-game chat messages
  • Inventory (dice skins, boosts, virtual currency)
  • Linked Google / Apple authentication tokens

What data is retained

The following data may be retained for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes:

  • In-app purchase transaction records — retained for up to 7 years as required by tax and consumer-protection laws.
  • Anonymized server logs — retained for up to 90 days for technical diagnostics and security.

Retained data is anonymized and cannot be linked back to you personally.

Partial data deletion

If you wish to delete only specific data (for example, chat history) without deleting your entire account, mention this in your email request and specify which data you want removed.

Contact

For any questions about your data or the deletion process, contact: monoplay.app@gmail.com
